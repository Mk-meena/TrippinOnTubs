Trippin on Tubs Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Trippin on Tubs, a mobile application available on iOS and Android platforms. This privacy policy aims to inform you about the information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Trippin on Tubs does not collect any personal information or device identifiers. Trippin on Tubs does collect diagnostic information, such as display resolution, system memory, and frame rate.

How We Use Information

The diagnostic information is solely used for quality assurance to improve your user experience and to identify potential issues with our application.

Information Sharing

We do not share any of the diagnostic information we collect with third parties.

User Choices

As we do not collect personal information, there are no user-specific choices for controlling data at this time.
